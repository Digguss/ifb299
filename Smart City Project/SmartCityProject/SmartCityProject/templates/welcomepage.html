Welcome to Smart City Brisbane !

This website holds all the information you need whether you are visiting the city, conducting business or studying within the city. We have designed the smart city website to show information you need depending on what type of account you create. All information will provide contact information, opening hours and direction information including map locations and pictures of the locations so you can easily find your destination. Please read below for more information on each account type.

Tourist
If this is your first time visiting welcome to Brisbane !
There are many sights and attractions to see in Brisbane, And we have included all the information you need from transport and getting around to restaraunts and hotels located in the city. For your convienance we have also included supermarkets and pharmacys. We have also included popular attractions and sightseeing locations both in the city and around the area that you can choose to visit while staying in Brisbane.

Business
A business account will provide you with the necessary tools to find hotels, companys and industrie buildings and their opening times aswell as contact information while also providing information about the city and local eaterys and restaraunts. Business accounts will also be be shown information about transport services avaliable in the city aswell as supermarkets and pharmacy locations.

Student
If you are from overseas and studying welcome to Brisbane !
A Student account will provide you with cheap accomodation and information on where to find local eaterys and restaraunts. If you need to get some study done we have also included libary locations and contact information to get all your studying done. We have also provided information on tranport services that you can use while in the city.
